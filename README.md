# Audit Finding Lab

## Overview

This project demonstrates a simulated internal information security review performed as part of a Governance, Risk and Compliance (GRC) portfolio.

The objective was to assess selected information security controls, identify weaknesses, document audit findings and recommend corrective actions.

The project was created to gain practical experience with audit activities, reporting, risk identification and corrective action management.

---

## Project Objectives

- Review information security controls
- Identify control weaknesses
- Assess associated risks
- Document audit findings
- Recommend corrective actions
- Track remediation activities

## Audit Findings

### AF001 – MFA Not Enabled

Risk:
Compromised credentials could result in unauthorized access.

Recommendation:
Enable Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA) for all users.

Priority:
High

---

### AF002 – Security Awareness Training Missing

Risk:
Employees may be vulnerable to phishing attacks.

Recommendation:
Implement annual security awareness training.

Priority:
High

---

### AF003 – Asset Register Review Not Performed

Risk:
Asset inventory may become inaccurate.

Recommendation:
Conduct annual asset register reviews.

Priority:
Medium

---

### AF004 – Backup Restoration Testing Not Documented

Risk:
Recovery procedures may fail during an incident.

Recommendation:
Perform and document backup restoration testing.

Priority:
High

---

### AF005 – Vulnerability Scanning Not Performed Regularly

Risk:
Known vulnerabilities may remain unpatched.

Recommendation:
Implement monthly vulnerability scanning.

Priority:
High
